## Project Brief

Gabay is a pedestrian safety navigation app designed for Filipino commuters who walk home at night. It addresses the problem of unsafe walking routes by offering a safety-first alternative to typical navigation apps that prioritize speed over protection. The app recommends safer routes based on lighting, foot traffic, nearby safe havens, and reported hazards, helping night-shift workers, students, and other pedestrians navigate more confidently and securely.

## Google Technologies Used

Gabay uses Google Maps Platform for map rendering and navigation, Vertex AI (Gemini) for threat analysis and classification, and Firebase Authentication for user-related flows. The app also integrates Google Cloud services to support its backend and data processing needs.
